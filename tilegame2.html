How to Play the Harry Potter Tile Game

The harry potter tile game takes all of the mechanics of the classic game: memory and throws them into the rich world of the Harry Potter series of novels for young prople.
Players have a limited number of turns to clear the board. Each turn they may flip over two tiles revealing Harry Potter themed artwork from the specific book that corosponds to their current level. Everytime they match two identical pictures the tiles, wil vanish as if by magic. When the board is fully cleared the option will appear to advance to the next level which will be more challenging and contain more tiles.
The game was created by Alicia Freedman and myself using Javascript, CSS and a HTML frame.
